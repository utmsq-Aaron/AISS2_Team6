VERIFICATION STATUS: CONFIRMED
NOTE on "acute load in both numerator and denominator": The ACWR = acute (1-week average) / chronic (4-week average). The 4-week chronic average includes the acute week, hence mathematical coupling. Figure 1 (p. 894) shows the Banister model equation and its oversimplification into the ACWR.
NOTE on our framing: Our paper correctly presents ACWR as "one contextual signal among several rather than a standalone injury predictor," which aligns with the source's recommendation to rely on clinical experience and traditional training principles rather than ACWR alone.
