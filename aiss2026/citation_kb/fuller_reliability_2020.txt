Claim: [CITE] systematically reviewed 158 publications covering 45 device models from nine manufacturers. For step counting under controlled conditions, 45% of 979 comparisons fell within +/-3% of criterion measures (median error: -2%). For heart rate, 57% of comparisons were within +/-3% error (Apple Watch: 71%; Fitbit: 51%; Garmin: 49%). Precision degraded during high-intensity exercise and in free-living settings.
