This directly confirms the "customisable, conversable agents" and "LLMs, tools, and humans in flexible conversation patterns" claims. Discussion (p.9): "The adoption of AutoGen has resulted in improved performance (over state-of-the-art approaches), reduced development code, and decreased manual burden."
